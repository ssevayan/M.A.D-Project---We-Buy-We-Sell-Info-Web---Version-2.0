    • Condition Guideline

    Manga

    1. Cover and Spine:
      • No major creases, tears, or folds.
      • Minimal wear along the edges.
      • The spine is intact and shows little to no signs of damage.

    2. Pages:
      • Pages are clean, without significant discoloration.
      • No writing, highlighting, or underlining on the pages.
      • Minimal signs of wear and tear on page edges.

    3. Binding:
      • Binding is tight, and pages are securely attached.
      • No loose or missing pages.

    4. Stickers and Labels:
      • No stickers, labels, or residue on the cover or pages.

    5. Odor:
      • Manga is free from unusual or unpleasant odors (e.g., smoke, mildew).

    6. Extras:
      • Any included extras, such as posters or inserts, are present and in good condition.

    7. Overall Wear:
      • Overall, the manga exhibits minimal wear consistent with careful handling.

    Figure

    1. Box Condition:
      • The original packaging should be intact with minimal signs of wear. A well-maintained box enhances the overall value and appeal.

    2. Figure Integrity:
      • The figure itself should be free from major defects such as cracks, chips, or broken parts. Ensure that all components and accessories are present and in good condition.

    3. Paint and Color:
      • The paintwork should be vibrant and well-preserved, without significant fading or discoloration. Check for any noticeable scratches or paint transfers.

    4. Articulation and Posability:
      • For articulated figures, confirm that joints are functional and not overly loose. Figures should be able to hold poses securely.

    Plush Toys

    1. Cleanliness:
      • Plush Toys are thoroughly cleaned, free of stains, and have no noticeable odors.

    2. Fabric Integrity:
      • The plushie's fabric remains intact without tears, rips, or fraying.

    3. Stitching:
      • All stitching is secure, ensuring the plushie's durability.

    4. Color Fading:
      • Minimal to no color fading on the plushie's exterior.

    5. Stability:
      • Plush Toys maintain their original shape and structure.

    6. No Missing Parts:
      • All parts (eyes, noses, accessories) are present and securely attached.

    7. Tag Presence:
      • Plush Toys must have CE logo or UKCA .

    8. Softness:
      • Plush Toys remain soft and huggable, providing a delightful tactile experience.
